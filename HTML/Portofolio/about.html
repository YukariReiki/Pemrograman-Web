About Me

My name is Muhammad Ilham Yumar, and I have been playing and modding games since 5th grade. I have been experimenting with Valve's GoldSource Engine games such as Half-Life, Day Of Defeat, and Counter-Strike.
I know a bit about the game's C++ Source code but I mostly work with the Game's Third Party Plugin AmxMod X scripting language, which is based on the PAWN language, and I enjoy using the Engine's Level Editor, "Valve Hammer Editor."

I also know how to create waypoints for community-made CPU players such as PODBot, YAPB, SYPB, and EBot.
I'm familiar with working on dedicated servers, specifically for Counter-Strike, as I used to configure local servers for myself and my friends to play with bots back when LAN parties were popular.
With this knowledge, many of my friends on Discord often ask me to set up their servers for playing.

I also had basic knowledge of Local Networking and Desktop Assembly and Maintenance.
